🔈 Installing the Hebrew Voice
🍎 iOS
iPhone & iPad
🤖 Android
All Android devices
🍎 iOS — iPhone & iPad
1

Open Settings

Go to your iPhone Settings app

2

Accessibility → Spoken Content

Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content

3

Tap "Voices"

Scroll down and tap the Voices option

4

Find Hebrew (עברית)

Scroll to find Hebrew and tap the download ⬇️ button to install the voice

5

Restart BrainTrainer

Close and reopen the app — Hebrew voice instructions will now work

🤖 Android
1

Open Settings

Go to your Android Settings app

2

Accessibility → Text-to-Speech output

Settings → Accessibility → Text-to-Speech output

3

Tap ⚙️ next to Google TTS Engine

Then tap "Install voice data"

4

Find Hebrew (עברית) and download

If Hebrew is not listed, search the Play Store for "Hebrew TTS" and install a Hebrew voice engine

5

Restart BrainTrainer

Close and reopen the app — Hebrew voice instructions will now work

🔒 Privacy & Data
📵

No Internet Required

BrainTrainer works fully offline. No data is ever sent to any server.

💾

Local Storage Only

Your game statistics and progress are stored only on your device.

👤

No Account Needed

No registration, no login, no personal information collected.

🚫

No Ads

BrainTrainer contains no advertisements of any kind.

🔒 BrainTrainer does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The only data stored is your game statistics, saved locally on your device and never transmitted.
